# 01 — Executive Summary

Complete specification for the renovation of **medalhero.com** into a premium, 3D scroll-interactive website.

## Goals

- Create a world-class, scroll-interactive 3D landing experience that converts visitors into app downloads
- Communicate all six messaging pillars through immersive visual storytelling
- Showcase the premium medal, environmental impact, and Japanese cultural challenges
- Deliver a seamless experience from desktop (1440px+) to mobile (320px)
- Achieve Lighthouse scores of 90+ across all categories
- Support bilingual content (Japanese primary, English secondary)

## Scope of Renovation

The current site at medalhero.com will be entirely replaced. Content is drawn from three existing pages: the homepage, `/about-app`, and `/contact`. The new site is a **single-page scroll experience** with distinct scroll-triggered sections. No subpages are needed — all content is unified into one seamless flow.
